## Creating a directory of successful designers

To enhance my skills and widen my network, I took the initiative to design and build a directory aimed at helping aspiring designers learn how to enter the industry. On this site, professional designers share their path to design and offer their advice along the way.

This is an ongoing project that I will constantly iterate and develop. It has become a platform for me to experiment with both design and implementation, allowing me to further hone my skills.
