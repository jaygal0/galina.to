As I was interested in exploring various skills and disciplines, I embarked on a personal project called Galiway. The idea was to document my journey as I learned different skills over a short period of time, ranging from language learning to coding and more.

This was my introduction to CMS, the art of blogging and building a personal brand.

### Closed

I decided to close this project as I felt I had achieved my initial goals and wanted to focus on new challenges. However, the experience gained from Galiway has been invaluable in shaping my approach to learning and personal development.
