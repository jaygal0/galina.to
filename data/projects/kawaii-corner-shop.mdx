Kawaii Corner Shop was an e-commerce store I created to celebrate and share my love for Japanese culture. The store featured a variety of products, including stationery, accessories, and home decor, all inspired by the cute and whimsical aesthetic of Japan. From the branding to running the Shopify store, I handled every aspect of the business.

### Closed

After running the store for a few months, and amassing a few sales. I decided to close it down to focus on other projects and opportunities. While the store is no longer active, it remains a cherished part of my journey as a designer and entrepreneur.
